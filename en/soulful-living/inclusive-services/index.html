Your soulful
package.

Once you arrive here, you can leave all your cares at the door. We take care of everything for you: from spacious studios and suites to a large-scale wellness area with saunas, onsen pool and rooftop terrace spa to alpine sports as well as yoga and qi gong. Additionally, we will also spoil you with a delicious breakfast and a five-course soul food dinner as well as inspiring readings and spoken-word sauna pours. 

Soulful living
Your retreat with an abundance of soul. Like yin and yang, our studios and suites complement each other: There are two paths – either you choose “the path of inspiration“, a bright and stimulating motif. Or you take the cosy and warm recreational path.

  • Private, cosy balcony or terrace
  • Inspiration or recreation welcome drink at the bar
  • Turndown service
  • Bathrobe and bathing bag in your studio or suite.
  • Kettle for your cup of tea
  • Care products from our in-house THE PURE SILENA care line
  • Some accommodation with coffee machine, own sauna, meditation space, tatami mat, yoga mat and outdoor bathtub
  • Free and open WLAN
  • Free parking space by the SILENA or in the garage. A garage parking space is included in some room categories; otherwise you are welcome to reserve your space for € 15 per night.

Soul Food
South Tyrol, our home, provides the basis for our dishes. South-east Asia is the chief inspiration for our flavours. So you can look forward to hearty South Tyrolean dishes and sophisticated tastes from South-east Asia.

  • Soulful breakfast with buffet and á la carte dishes in the restaurant and on the terrace too in summertime.
  • Five-course soul food menu in the evening
  • Vegan and vegetarian options
  • Fresh home-grown ingredients or from local farmers.
  • Any food intolerances or special dietary requirements? That's no problem. Our kitchen team is supremely well-prepared and trained.

Spa area
You’ve arrived. At our hotel and in yourself. In our soulful spa area, you can let your soul be free. Savour the warmth in our saunas, feel weightless in our rooftop onsen pool or allow your thoughts to drift in our rest rooms.

  • Relaxation in our moor hideaway on the ground floor and in our rooftop hideaway on the roof terrace. Both areas are connected with a separate lift.
  • Saunas for everyone: In our organic herb sauna (Herbaria) with the fragrance of moorland herbs you perspire at a pleasant temperature of 45 - 60 degrees and 30 - 55 per cent humidity. In the Finnish sauna (Finnaria) on the roof you will experience a temperature of 80 - 100 degrees and 2 - 5 per cent humidity. The steam bath (Woodaria) with high humidity is a little earthier.
  • Various rest rooms indoors and outdoors offer plenty of space, enabling you to find your completely private retreat
  • Roof-top onsen pool and onsen rest room
  • Indoor pool and outdoor pool with indoor access
  • Spacious sun garden with sunbathing lawn

Literature & tea

  • Reading pleasure pure: Library with 1,000 volumes
  • Coffee table books, theme novels and text books on South Tyrol
  • Tea house with countless teas from South Tyrol and South-East Asia
  • Own SILENA tea line with individually produced teas
  • Kettle and tea in your studio or suite

Tea
Tea calms, grounds and strengthens. Get to know the exciting world of the tea ritual at the SILENA. An interpretation from South Tyrol and Asia.

  • Teahouse with countless teas from South Tyrol and Asia
  • In-house SILENA tea line with individually produced teas
  • Kettle and tea in your studio or suite.
  • Tea pots and service from South Tyrol and Asia

Summer activities 2023
In the summer, you can expect a sea of blossoms, green meadows and blue skies. At an altitude of 1,354m you’ll have plenty of ideas for sports activities.

  • At the heart of the countryside yet close to where it’s happening
  • Hiking, biking or winter activities directly from the SILENA
  • Situated close to the valley station of the Rio di Pusteria alpine and ski region.
  • We take guests on guided hikes five times a week
  • Free bike hire, e-bikes for a fee
  • Participation in your daily yoga session
  • Participation in a top-quality programme of mountain sports activities, gentle movement and Qi Gong and breathing hikes
  • Use of the SILENA fitness room with Technogym® equipment
  • Personal training on request and for a fee

Winter activities 2023
Snow-covered mountains, cloud-white landscapes. Spend your winter's day as you like.

  • Ski-in and ski-out: Situated close to the valley station of the Rio di Pusteria alpine and ski region’s cable cars
  • Skiing, snowboarding, cross-country, tobogganing and winter hiking directly from the SILENA.
  • You can stow all your equipment securely with us in your personal ski locker in our ski cellar, which also has a ski boot drier
  • We take guests on guided hikes five times a week
  • Participation in your daily yoga session
  • Participation in a top-quality programme of mountain sports activities, gentle movement and Qi Gong along with inspiring meditation suggestions
  • Use of the SILENA fitness room with Technogym® equipment
  • Personal training on request and for a fee

Almencard
Ready to go at 1354 metres above sea level? We have a fantastic bonus for you! As our guest you will receive a free Almencard as a gift when you check in.

  • Free travel on all regional public transport.
  • Participation in the Rio di Pusteria Tourism Association's weekly programme
  • Free entry to over 90 museums and exhibitions in South Tyrol.
  • From 13.05.2023 until 07.07.2023 and 18.09.2023 until 04.11.2023 you are also entitled to travel free with the mountain lifts of South Tyrol (the Gitschberg and Jochtal gondola lift, the Mühlbach-Meransen, Bozen-Ritten, Jenesien, Burgstall-Vöran, and Mölten-Vilipan funiculars, the Ritten tramcar and Mendel railway).

Transfer
Your car is on holiday. You can easily reach us by bus or train. At the SILENA you are surrounded by nature yet close to the action and can also easily explore the region without a car.

  • Situated close to the valley station of the Rio di Pusteria alpine and ski region
  • Free rail transfer from Mühlbach/Rio di Pusteria or Franzensfeste/Fortezza. Please let us know by 12.00 a.m. on the previous day at the latest so that we can arrange your transfer
  • You can also arrange to be picked up from other railway stations or airports on request for a charge
